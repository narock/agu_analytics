Help us disambiguate the multiple sources of people data. Some datasets use first initial and last name. Others use full names. When combining these data for our analytics it's difficult to infer if two people who appear similar are in fact the same. We're using ORCID identifiers to uniquely identify the people in our dataset. If you have an ORCID please supply it below. In addition, email addresses can also be helpful in identifying people. However, email addresses can change. Please also supply a list of email addresses you may have used in the past. The data you supply will be used by our software to automatically align people across datasets.
